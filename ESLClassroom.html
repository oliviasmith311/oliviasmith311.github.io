Our ESL Classroom

In this project, I worked on a team to create a messaging app to create an easier mode of communication between ESL parents and their children's teachers. The app allowed teachers and parents to message one another in their native language and displayed the messages on the other end in the receiver's spoken language.

Skills used: Java, Spring, Hibernate, JPA, HTML, CSS, Agile (Scrum), TDD, OOP, JSON
